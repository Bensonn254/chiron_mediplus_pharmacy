Chiron Mediplus Privacy Policy

This Policy Was Last Updated October 01, 2024.

Information Collection

We collect the information you choose to share with us, such as your name, contact details, prescription details, and delivery address when applicable.

This helps us verify prescriptions, fulfill orders, respond to inquiries, and provide safe pharmacy services.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process orders, communicate about refills or deliveries, and provide medication counseling and support.

We do not sell your personal data. We only use it for pharmacy operations and service improvement.

Sharing Your Information

We may share information with trusted service providers who help us deliver medicines or operate our systems, only as needed to provide services.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect patient safety.

Medical Disclaimer

Content on this site is for general information and does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

If you have a medical emergency, contact your healthcare provider or emergency services.

Data Protection

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your personal data and limit access to authorized staff only.

While we work hard to protect your information, no system is completely secure.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us remember preferences and understand how visitors use our site so we can improve the experience.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

Your Rights and Choices

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal data where applicable. Contact us if you have questions or need assistance.

We will respond to privacy requests within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable laws.

Chiron Mediplus Pharmacy provides trusted medicines, clear guidance, and friendly service for your family.
