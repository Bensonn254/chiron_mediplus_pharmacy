FAQ

Pharmacy Questions, Answered

  • Can I upload a prescription online?

    Yes. Use the upload button and we will verify your prescription and prepare your medicines for pickup or delivery.

  • Do you offer home delivery in Kitengela?

    We offer convenient local delivery within Kitengela and nearby areas. Contact us to confirm coverage and delivery fees.

  • Do you provide blood pressure or glucose checks?

    Yes, we offer quick in-pharmacy screenings during opening hours, with simple guidance on your results.

  • Can you check for drug interactions?

    Our pharmacists review prescriptions to help prevent interactions and advise on safe, effective use.

  • Do you stock over-the-counter products?

    We carry a wide range of OTC medicines, vitamins, and wellness essentials. Ask our team for recommendations.
